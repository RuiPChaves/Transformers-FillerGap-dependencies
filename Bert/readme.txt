Bert_Agreement.zip contains additional experiments, in which the original items are embedded in more complex structures. 

In Experiment 1 the subject NPs preceeding the clause with a filler-gap dependency of interest have 3 to 4 words, and BERT's ability to keep track of the filler-gap dependency downstream is unaffected.

In Experiment 2 the subject NPs preceeding the clause with the filler-gap dependency of interest have 5 to 15 words, and BERT's ability to keep track of the filler-gap dependency downstream is hampered, but only in embedding 2 and 4...
